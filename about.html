About Me

My Background


Growing up in a digital age, I have always been surrounded by technology. I still remember getting my first iPod like it was yesterday. I found technology fascinating and knew that I wanted to pursue a career in the field later down the line. I had only ever been the user of cutting-edge technologies like the iPod, Wii, or Alexa, and I never understood how the devices themselves worked. That changed in high school when I took my first Computer Science class which introduced me to coding.


This class taught coding fundamentals in Scratch and Python. Although coding was a confusing concept at first, I fell in love with the ability to create something with just a few simple lines of code. My passion for engineering and technology was solidified by other High School classes that I took that taught Web Design and Electronics basics. I decided to apply for Computer Science and Engineering programs around the East Coast, and now here I am a few years later ready to graduate with a degree in Computer Engineering!


Present Day


I am currently a Senior at Virginia Tech and will be graduating this May with a Bachelors of Science in Computer Engineering. I also have a Minor in Computer Science and a Focus in Networking & Cybersecurity. Outside of my coursework, I am a member of Beta Theta Pi, the National Society of Leadership and Success, and IEEE@VT. In my free time, I enjoy going to the gym and exercising, playing golf, watching sports (Go Hokies!) and spending time with friends and family.


This past summer, I worked as an Intern for Thorben Consulting LLC. During my summer at Thorben, I worked on an intern team that learned how use the AWS Management Console to develop, debug, and deploy cloud-based applications that follow AWS best practices. I was also able to acheive two AWS Certifications through the AWS Partner Network: the AWS Cloud Practitioner and the AWS Developer Associate. I look forward to joining a company full-time after graduation that will give me the opportunity to work with software solutions that make meaningful impacts on people's lives.
